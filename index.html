Who We Are

We Read the Lore.
We Took the Side Quest.

Kessel Logistics Run is a highsec corporation for people who showed up to New Eden, looked at the nullsec blocs, the sov warfare, the 23-hour sieges and the 400-page coalition politics documents — and decided that wasn't why they logged in.

We're gamers. Nerds. Sci-fi fans, lore readers, bad decision makers, and occasional competent pilots. We have opinions about Star Wars — strong opinions — and one member who is wrong about Star Wars but we keep them around anyway.

The fleet that forms up, warps to the wrong gate, and calls it a success. The mining op that turns into a two-hour discussion about whether Han shot first. The guy who spent three weeks theorycrafting a fit he'll never use. That's us.

We Do

  • Missions (the storyline agents deserve an audience)
  • Mining, socially, with music and bad takes
  • Exploration, when we remember it exists
  • Industry, slowly, with spreadsheets (yes there are spreadsheets)
  • Whatever looked interesting on the map tonight
  • Very long discussions about sci-fi canon

We Don't

  • Mandatory ops
  • CTAs at 3am
  • Drama
  • Nullsec (we've seen the forums)
  • Gatekeeping your playstyle
  • Taking this too seriously

If you're a returning player who forgot most of the UI, a new player who has no idea what's happening, or a veteran who is tired of everything being a job — you'll fit in fine.
